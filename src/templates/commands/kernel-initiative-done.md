Mark a strategic initiative as done.

This updates the initiative status to "done" and records the completion time.

Use when:
- The strategic initiative is complete
- The theme is no longer active
- Ready to close out the initiative

Note: This does not delete the initiative — it remains accessible in the archive for history.
